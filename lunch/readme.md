# Cheese Quesadilla

## Servings: 2

## Time to prepare: 10 minutes

## Ingredients: 
- tortillas
- cheese
- cooking oil (vegetable oil)


## Equipment needed:
- stove
- pan
- spatula
- plate
- fork
- knife


## Steps for preparation:
- put pan on stove
- turn stove on
- add tortilla to pan
- add cheese
- turn stove off
- put quesadilla on plate
