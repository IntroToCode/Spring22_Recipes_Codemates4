# Mac and cheese 

## Servings: 3

## Time to prepare: 15 mins

## Ingredients:
100ml water.
KRAFT Macaroni & Cheese Dinner.
1 stick margarine.
1 cup milk.

## Equipment needed:
Pot

## Steps for preparation:
Put Water
Boil Water
Put Kraft
Add a half scoop of sugar
Put milk and margarine
Mix
Serve
