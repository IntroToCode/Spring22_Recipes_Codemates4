# Mac and cheese 

## Servings: 3

## Time to prepare: 15 mins

## Ingredients:
100ml water.
KRAFT Macaroni & Cheese Dinner.
1 stick margarine.
1 cup milk.

## Equipment needed:
Pot
Wooden Spoon (or other stirring utensil of choice)

## Steps for preparation:

Put Water
Boil Water
Put Kraft
Put milk and margarine
Mix
Serve
